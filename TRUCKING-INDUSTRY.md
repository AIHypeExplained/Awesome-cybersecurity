# Cybersecurity in the Trucking Industry

This document covers cybersecurity challenges, best practices, and resources specific to the trucking and logistics sector.

## Overview

The trucking industry relies on connected vehicles, telematics, mobile apps, and logistics systems. Securing these systems is critical to protect fleets, drivers, cargo, and customer data.

## Key Topics

- Fleet security and telematics protection
- Supply chain vulnerability management
- Secure IoT and onboard systems
- Driver safety and data privacy
- Incident response for transportation networks

## What to Look For in a Trucking Pentest Provider

- Experience with ELD, TMS, WMS, dispatch portals, telematics, APIs, and mobile driver apps
- Ability to test both enterprise IT and vehicle/embedded surfaces
- Safe methodology for live fleet assets so testing does not disrupt operations
- Familiarity with transportation/logistics and connected-truck threat models
